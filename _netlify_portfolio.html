Customer Churn Prediction Dashboard

20.38%

Churn Rate Identified

Built comprehensive Power BI dashboard analyzing 10,000+ customer records to identify churn patterns, risk factors, and actionable retention strategies across demographics and product segments.

Power BI Data Analytics Dashboard Design
The Problem

XYZ Bank needed to understand customer churn patterns to implement targeted retention strategies. With 10,000 customers and a 20.38% churn rate, identifying at-risk segments was critical.

The Approach

Analyzed customer dataset across multiple dimensions including demographics, product usage, satisfaction, and behavioral metrics. Created interactive Power BI dashboard with drill-down capabilities.

Key Findings

25.07%

Insights Generated
  • Geographic Risk: Germany shows significantly higher churn (32.44%) compared to Spain (16.67%) and France (16.17%)
  • Gender Disparity: Female customers churn at 25.07% vs. 16.47% for males—25% higher risk
  • Product Impact: Customers with 1 product show highest churn (1409 customers), suggesting need for product engagement strategies
  • Satisfaction Correlation: Satisfaction score directly correlates with churn—score of 2 shows peak churn at 22%
  • Complaint Pattern: Zero complaints = fewer customers; one complaint = exponential churn risk (linear growth to 2000+ customers)
  • Age Vulnerability: Peak churn concentration in 40-50 age group with secondary spike at younger ages
Recommendations
  • Implement Germany-specific retention program with localized service improvements
  • Create female-focused engagement strategy addressing unique pain points
  • Design multi-product incentives to increase customer stickiness beyond single product
  • Establish complaint escalation protocol—complaints are churn predictors
  • Develop age-segmented retention campaigns for 40-50 age group
  • Proactive satisfaction monitoring with interventions at satisfaction score drops
Dashboard Features
  • Real-time churn rate KPI tracking
  • Interactive filtering by country, gender, product count, and satisfaction score
  • Trend analysis showing complaints vs. churn correlation
  • Age distribution with churn overlay for demographic targeting
  • Segmentation matrix for multi-variable analysis
  • Drill-down capability for investigation at customer group level
